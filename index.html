Billionares Analysis visualisation

In this project, I take a closer look at the world’s billionaires;not just how much they’re worth, but where they come from, how they built their fortunes, and how their wealth is spread across the globe. I explore patterns in industries and countries where billionaire wealth is most concentrated, and dive into demographics like age, gender, and birthplace to see who’s really topping the rich lists. I also look at whether billionaires are self-made or inherited their wealth, and how their fortunes relate to broader economic factors like GDP and tax rates. With visual maps and trends over time, this analysis paints a clearer picture of how global wealth is evolving—and what it might tell us about the world today.
